About The Operation

The story behind the steel

West Steel From Old People began as a simple question: "What if those brightly-boxed brick retail chains had a gritty, industrial cousin who worked at a scrapyard?" The answer was obvious.

We provide premium metallic building elements for adults who want their creations to feel like they could support actual structural weight. Our parts are 97% compatible with major brick brands — the other 3% is just personality.

Every purchase funds our charity: "Grandparents Gone Scrapy", a proudly independent organization dedicated to helping seniors understand why their toolboxes keep getting lighter. (Not actually a charity. Or independent. We're three guys in a garage.)
